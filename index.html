Quote Cards
As someone who was tired of manually creating index cards for English research papers, I developed a Chrome extension called Quote Cards. It enables users to highlight text on any website and generate a digital index card for the quote. The cards are automatically saved to the user's local storage, and the they can easily search through their deck of cards to find earlier quotes. Quote Cards simplifies the process of organizing and referencing research material for academic papers.
